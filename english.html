Research

Research Interests

My research interest is human language processing, especially visual word recognition of Japanese Kanji and Kana words. We currently work on projects such as semantic similarity and transparency of Kanji character words, Japanese word search system, second language processing, and homophone processing using visual world paradigm with co-researchers.
